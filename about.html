About Kutz & Kolorz


Kutz & Kolorz is a family oriented hair salon and has been open for four years,
nearing its 5th annniversary. Kutz & Kolorz offers a variety of services including
hair cuts for all genders, color services, and waxing. Kutz & Kolorz also sells a variety of
products from brands slike OLAPEX, Surface, and Matrix.
